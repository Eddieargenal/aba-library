# Build Back Better Recovery Alignment Tool

## Purpose

Aligns post-disaster recovery and reconstruction decisions with Build Back Better (BBB) principles to ensure recovery investment reduces future disaster risk rather than recreating pre-disaster vulnerabilities. Provides an integration checklist for ABA teams working at the recovery/transition phase.

## BBB Principles

<!-- TODO[agent]: Define each principle with ABA-specific application and evidence standard -->

1. Risk-informed reconstruction (build to higher standard)
2. Inclusive recovery (do not exclude marginalised groups)
3. Local ownership (community and municipal leadership)
4. Economic resilience (livelihood recovery integrated)
5. Environmental sustainability (green infrastructure, ecosystem services)
