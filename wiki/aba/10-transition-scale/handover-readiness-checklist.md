# Handover Readiness Checklist

## Purpose

Provides a structured checklist to assess whether conditions for handing over an area-based programme to local government or community structures are met. Prevents premature exit that leaves communities at renewed risk, and prevents indefinite humanitarian presence that undermines local ownership.

## Checklist Domains

<!-- TODO[agent]: For each item, add assessment method, evidence source, and minimum threshold for pass -->

### Governance Readiness
- [ ] Municipal authority engaged and formally accepting responsibility
- [ ] Local DRR or emergency management structure in place
- [ ] Budget line or resource commitment confirmed
- [ ] Legal or policy framework supporting handover exists

### Technical Capacity
- [ ] Local staff trained in ongoing monitoring methods
- [ ] Data systems transferred and accessible to local team
- [ ] Maintenance protocols for infrastructure in place
- [ ] Community preparedness groups functional

### Programme Outcomes
- [ ] Target vulnerability indicators at or below threshold
- [ ] Local service functionality at pre-crisis or better baseline
- [ ] Evidence-gaps-to-fill addressed or formally accepted as residual risk

### Community Readiness
- [ ] Community representation in handover planning confirmed
- [ ] Feedback mechanism in place post-handover
- [ ] Protection risks assessed and mitigated
