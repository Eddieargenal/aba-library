# Known Tensions Index (v2.7)

## Section Description

- What: Known-tension pages (`KTN-`) documenting unresolved or context-dependent tradeoffs.
- Why: Makes disagreement explicit so agents do not produce false consensus.
- When: Use when sources conflict or multiple valid approaches exist.
- How: Describe both sides, decision conditions, and required caveats.
- Whom: Reviewers, synthesis agents, and humans approving high-risk recommendations.
