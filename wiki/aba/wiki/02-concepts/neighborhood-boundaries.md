# Definition
The delineated perimeter of an operational area for ABA programming, which may be based on administrative boundaries, community identity, service catchment areas, hazard zones, or a combination of criteria.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
Boundary definition affects who is included or excluded from the program, which services are accessible, and how coordination is organized — administrative boundaries may exclude key populations while community-defined boundaries may be socially meaningful but operationally difficult.
