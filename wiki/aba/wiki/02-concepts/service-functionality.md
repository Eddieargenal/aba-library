# Definition
The operational status of urban services (water, sanitation, health, electricity, markets, transport) within a defined area — whether they are functioning, degraded, disrupted, or destroyed — and the barriers that prevent access for different population groups.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
ABA programs must diagnose service functionality to decide whether to restore, substitute, supplement, or reform services rather than duplicate them — failing to assess functionality leads to parallel service systems that undermine sustainability.
