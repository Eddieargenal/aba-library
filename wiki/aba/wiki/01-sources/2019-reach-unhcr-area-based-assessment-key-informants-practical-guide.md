# Summary

This REACH and UNHCR practical guide provides step-by-step methodology for conducting area-based assessments using key informant interviews, addressing sampling strategy, respondent selection, questionnaire design, field management, data quality assurance, and analysis. It is designed for practitioners who need robust, reliable area-level data without household survey resources. The guide is the primary methodological source for the area profile, needs prioritization, and area reassessment tools in this wiki.
