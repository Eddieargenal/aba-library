# Summary

This 2026 IASC document establishes standard terms of reference for area-based coordination mechanisms, providing the most current normative framework for how humanitarian actors should organize coordination at the area/neighborhood level. It defines the mandate, membership, governance, decision-making, and accountability structures appropriate for area-based coordination platforms. This is the authoritative source for coordination architecture design in the wiki, superseding older guidance.
