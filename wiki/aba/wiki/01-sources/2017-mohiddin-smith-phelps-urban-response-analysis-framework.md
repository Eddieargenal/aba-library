# Summary

The URAF guidance note presents the Urban Response Analysis Framework developed by the Stronger Cities Consortium, providing humanitarian practitioners with a structured approach to analyzing urban response options across modalities including cash transfers, in-kind assistance, direct works, referrals, advocacy, and technical assistance. It enables teams to compare options systematically across criteria including feasibility, impact, cost-effectiveness, market effects, and sustainability. The URAF is the core tool for Stage 5 (integrated area strategy) and Stage 6 (program design and modality selection).
