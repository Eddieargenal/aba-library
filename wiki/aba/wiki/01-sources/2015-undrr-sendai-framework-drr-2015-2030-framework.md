# Summary

The Sendai Framework is the global 15-year instrument (2015–2030) for reducing disaster risk and losses, adopted by UN Member States. It establishes four priorities: understanding risk, strengthening risk governance, investing in resilience, and enhancing preparedness for Build Back Better. The framework provides the international policy anchor for urban DRR programming, requiring humanitarian and development programs to demonstrate alignment with national and local risk reduction goals.
