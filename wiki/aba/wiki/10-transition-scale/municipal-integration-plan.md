# Municipal Integration Plan

## Purpose

Guides the integration of ABA humanitarian programme outcomes into municipal DRR planning, budgeting, and governance systems. Ensures that resilience gains from the programme are institutionalised rather than lost at programme closure.

## Integration Pathways

<!-- TODO[agent]: Map each ABA outcome domain to the relevant municipal system, actor, and policy instrument -->

1. **Risk information** → Municipal risk register, urban planning department
2. **Coordination structures** → Emergency management office, cluster/sector mainstreaming
3. **Community preparedness** → Local government community liaison, civil protection
4. **Infrastructure maintenance** → Public works, utility authorities
5. **Funding** → Municipal budget cycle, contingency funds, national DRR allocations
