# Operational Feasibility Checklist

## Purpose
A structured checklist for assessing operational feasibility of implementing an ABA program in the target area — covering access, staffing, time, data quality, systems capacity, protection safeguards, and exit planning. Feeds directly into Domain 9 scoring in Tool #01.

## When to use
Stage 0 (appropriateness decision). Must be completed before finalizing ABA decision.

## Who collects / facilitates
Program manager and team leader jointly, drawing on field team input.

## Respondents / participants
Internal team assessment. No external respondents.

## Enumerator / facilitator guidance
Be honest about capacity constraints. A low operational feasibility score is not a failure — it is an honest signal to not over-commit or to address prerequisites before proceeding.

## Data quality checks
- Both program manager and team leader must review and agree on final ratings
- Any NO on data protection or exit planning is a hard red flag
- Review operational feasibility checklist again at 30-day program mark to detect emerging issues

## Analysis use
Score Domain 9 (operational feasibility) in Tool #01 directly from this checklist summary.

## Risks / ethical safeguards
- Under-assessing operational feasibility leads to programs that harm communities through abandonment or poor quality
- Organizational pressure to show programs can proceed must not override honest assessment
- Document this checklist as evidence for program design decisions
