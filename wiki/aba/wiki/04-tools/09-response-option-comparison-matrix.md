# Tool 09: Response Option Comparison Matrix

## Purpose
A matrix for systematically comparing response options (cash, in-kind, direct works, referral, advocacy, technical assistance) across sectors to identify the most appropriate combination for the area strategy. Assesses market functionality, local capacity, and outcome probability for each modality.

## Decision questions
- Is cash more appropriate than in-kind given market functionality in this area?
- Should shelter response use direct construction or conditional cash transfers?
- When should needs be addressed through referral rather than direct implementation?
