# Tool 07: Community Engagement Platform Tool

## Purpose
A tool for designing and establishing a community engagement platform that enables meaningful participation of affected communities — including women, youth, and marginalized groups — in assessment, priority-setting, and program design. Defines participation mechanisms, safe spaces, feedback loops, and inclusion safeguards.

## Decision questions
- What participation mechanism is appropriate for this context?
- How can marginalized groups (women, youth, disabled, minority) participate safely?
- What feedback and accountability mechanisms are needed?
