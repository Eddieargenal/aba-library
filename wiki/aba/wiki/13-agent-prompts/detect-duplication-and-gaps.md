# Detect Duplication and Gaps

## When to use
Before finalizing area strategy to identify overlap and uncovered needs.

## Steps
1. Read [[./08coordination/4w-5w-management.md]]
2. Read [[./08coordination/duplication-detection.md]]
3. Collect actor/5W data from field
4. For each activity: identify who is doing what, where, when, for whom
5. Flag overlaps: same activity, same area, same beneficiary group, different actors
6. Flag gaps: needs identified with no actor covering them
7. Cross-reference with [[./08coordination/local-resource-leverage.md]] to identify what local actors are already doing
8. Produce: duplication list, gap list, recommended coordination actions
9. File synthesis to outputs/decision-memos/
10. Update [[./12risks-contradictions/evidence-gaps-to-fill.md]] if gaps involve missing evidence
