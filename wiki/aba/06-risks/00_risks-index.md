# Risks Index (v2.7)

## Section Description

- What: Canonical risk pages (`R-`) capturing failure modes, caveats, and mitigation actions.
- Why: Prevents unsafe or overconfident advisory outputs.
- When: Use during design review, advisory drafting, and pre-release risk checks.
- How: Record indicators, escalation triggers, and mitigations linked to evidence.
- Whom: Risk reviewers, technical leads, and operational decision-makers.
