<a id="risk-description"></a>
## Risk Description

The Characteristics were written for **natural hazards** and assume a degree of **community consensus**. In conflict and post-conflict settings — fragmented groups, contested authority, active insecurity — those assumptions may not hold, and **no field experience exists** to confirm the framework transfers. Applying it unadapted risks invalid analysis and harm.

<a id="indicators"></a>
## Indicators

Fragmented or contested community structures; absence of safe consensus-building space; the framework being applied in a setting with active or recent armed conflict and no conflict-specific adaptation.

<a id="mitigation"></a>
## Mitigation

Do not apply the Characteristics unadapted in conflict settings. Require conflict-sensitivity and do-no-harm review before use; treat any conflict-setting application as an explicit human-review (Gate B) decision. The adaptation evidence base is a **known gap** to be filled before promotion beyond draft.
