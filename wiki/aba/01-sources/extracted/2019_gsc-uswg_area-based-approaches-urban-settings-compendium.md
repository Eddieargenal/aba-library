# Summary

This Global Shelter Cluster and Urban Settlements Working Group compendium brings together case studies of area-based approaches across multiple urban crisis contexts, documenting how practitioners have designed, implemented, and evaluated area-based programs. It covers settlement and neighborhood delineation methodologies, area strategy development, sector-integrated intervention package design, outcome documentation, and lessons for scale-up and replication. The compendium is the most comprehensive practitioner evidence base for ABA in urban settings and is referenced across six matrix tools.
