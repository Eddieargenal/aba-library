# Summary

This UNDRR handbook provides local government leaders with practical guidance for building city resilience using the Ten Essentials framework, which covers risk governance, urban planning, ecological safeguards, critical infrastructure, assessment and preparedness, and recovery systems. It enables humanitarian and development practitioners to assess municipal DRR capacity during area selection and to design technical interventions aligned with municipal resilience goals. The Ten Essentials framework is internationally recognized and aligns with the Sendai Framework.
