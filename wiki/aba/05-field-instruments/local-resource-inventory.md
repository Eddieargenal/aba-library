# Local Resource Inventory

## Purpose
A structured inventory of local resources — human, material, institutional, financial, social — that exist within the target area and can be identified, engaged, and leveraged in the area strategy. Prevents external actors from duplicating what local systems can provide.

## When to use
Stage 2 (area profile) and Stage 5 (integrated area strategy design).

## Who collects / facilitates
Field officer consolidates from KIIs with community leaders, CBOs, service providers, and observation.

## Respondents / participants
No direct respondents — synthesis form from multiple KII and observation sources.

## Enumerator / facilitator guidance
Conduct this inventory with an explicit lens of 'what already exists that we should build on rather than duplicate?' Include informal resources — informal water vendors, community savings groups, traditional conflict resolution — not just formal institutions.

## Data quality checks
- Cross-check claimed resources with direct observation where possible
- Flag resources that are claimed but unverified
- Update as program progresses — resources emerge and disappear over time

## Analysis use
Score Domain 6 (local capacity/resource leverage) in Tool #01. Feed into integrated area strategy (Tool #10) to identify leverage opportunities.

## Risks / ethical safeguards
- Do not publicize financial resources (savings groups) that could attract theft
- Be sensitive to community leaders who may claim to represent local capacity but are gatekeepers
- Verify community institution functionality before committing to building on it
