# Service Functionality Mapping Sheet

## Purpose
A structured matrix for systematically mapping the pre-crisis and current functionality of all major urban services in the target area. Produces a service functionality baseline used in the urban systems diagnosis and area strategy design.

## When to use
Stage 2 (area profile and systems diagnosis). Complete after KIIs with service providers and before finalizing systems diagnosis.

## Who collects / facilitates
Field team consolidates data from multiple sources (observation, KII, secondary data) into this sheet.

## Respondents / participants
No direct respondents — synthesis sheet populated from multiple instruments.

## Enumerator / facilitator guidance
Use multiple evidence sources for each row. Note confidence level honestly — weak evidence rows should not anchor system status judgments. Return to update as new data arrives.

## Data quality checks
- Every row should cite at least 1 source
- Rows rated H (high confidence) must have at least 2 independent source types
- Review and update after each new KII or observation session

## Analysis use
Use to score Domain 4 (urban systems interdependence) in Tool #01 and to populate the urban systems diagnosis in Tool #04.

## Risks / ethical safeguards
- Do not treat partial evidence as complete service status
- Service functionality can change rapidly — date each update
- Do not share with actors who may exploit information about failed governance systems
