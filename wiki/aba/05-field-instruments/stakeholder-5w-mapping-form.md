# Stakeholder 5W Mapping Form

## Purpose
A structured 5W (Who, What, Where, When, for Whom) mapping form for cataloging all actors operating in the target area, their activities, geographic coverage, target populations, and timelines. Feeds directly into coordination gap and duplication analysis.

## When to use
Stage 2 (stakeholder mapping) and ongoing during implementation for coordination updates.

## Who collects / facilitates
Coordinator or program officer consolidates data from KIIs, coordination meetings, and secondary data.

## Respondents / participants
No direct respondents — synthesis form populated from KII data and coordination sources.

## Enumerator / facilitator guidance
Update after every coordination meeting. Share with all coordination platform members (with permission of data providers). Use the duplication-gap-analysis-matrix to process this data.

## Data quality checks
- Verify coverage claims with observation before using in analysis
- Mark any actor not directly interviewed as 'unverified' in the confidence column
- Require organizations to confirm their own row before treating as verified

## Analysis use
Score Domain 5 (stakeholder/coordination complexity) in Tool #01. Feed into duplication-gap-analysis-matrix and area coordination dashboard (Tool #13).

## Risks / ethical safeguards
- Do not share beneficiary numbers that could enable targeting for exploitation
- Store securely — contains actor presence information that could have security implications
- Get consent from organizations before sharing their data in coordination platforms
