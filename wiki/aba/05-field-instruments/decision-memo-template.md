# Decision Memo Template

## Purpose
A structured memo template for documenting the ABA appropriateness decision — summarizing assessment evidence, scoring, red flags, and the final recommendation. This memo is the required output of Tool #01 and the formal record of the ABA decision.

## When to use
At the end of Stage 0 (appropriateness decision), after completing all Tool #01 modules. Must be signed off by program manager before proceeding to area selection.

## Who collects / facilitates
Program manager or team leader authors; requires review and sign-off from a second senior staff member.

## Respondents / participants
Not applicable — documentation template.

## Enumerator / facilitator guidance
This memo is a required output. Do not proceed to area selection without a completed and signed decision memo. If the decision is to NOT proceed with ABA, document this carefully — the record protects the organization and demonstrates accountability.

## Data quality checks
- Every domain score must have evidence cited in the supporting instruments
- Weak evidence domains must be explicitly flagged
- Red flag review must be completed before recommendation is written
- Must be reviewed by a second person

## Analysis use
This is the output document of Tool #01, not an input. It synthesizes all domain scores and evidence into a decision record.

## Risks / ethical safeguards
- Do not allow scoring to become a justification exercise for a predetermined decision
- If pressure exists to show a positive ABA determination, document that pressure and escalate if necessary
- Retain memo for minimum 3 years after program end
