# Actor Responsibility Matrix

## Purpose
A matrix for clarifying actor responsibilities within the area program — specifying who leads, who supports, who monitors, and who coordinates for each activity domain. Prevents ambiguity and supports coordination accountability.

## Instructions
1. Complete after initial 5W mapping and coordination meeting
2. Review and update at each coordination cycle
3. Flag gaps where no lead actor is assigned
4. Flag conflicts where multiple actors claim the lead role
