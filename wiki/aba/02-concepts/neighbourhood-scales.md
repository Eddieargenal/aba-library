<a id="definition"></a>
## Definition

Neighbourhoods operate at two complementary scales with different functions: proximity scale (100–1,500 people, social coordination, mutual aid) and strategic scale (5,000–10,000 people, service infrastructure). Both scales should be used in parallel.
