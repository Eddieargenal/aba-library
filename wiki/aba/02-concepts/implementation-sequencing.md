# Definition
The logical ordering of program activities based on technical dependencies, community readiness, seasonal factors, actor coordination requirements, and resource availability to maximize cumulative impact and avoid bottlenecks.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
Poor sequencing in urban ABA programs creates wasted resources, beneficiary confusion, and coordination failures — especially when infrastructure, services, and livelihoods are interdependent and interventions must be sequenced to unlock subsequent outcomes.
