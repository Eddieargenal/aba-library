<a id="trigger"></a>
## Trigger Conditions

Field staff or community members must learn a complex resilience framework and risk disengaging. *"How do I train field staff on a complex resilience framework without losing them?"*

<a id="decision-logic"></a>
## Decision Logic

**Rule:** start from **what people already know** — real, familiar projects — and work **backwards** to the Characteristics, rather than teaching the framework top-down as an abstract taxonomy.

<a id="escalation"></a>
## Escalation

Escalate to the capacity-building lead where language/translation barriers or facilitation-skill gaps block the backwards-mapping approach.
