# Community Resilience Indicators

## Purpose

Catalogues validated indicators for measuring community-level disaster resilience in urban ABA contexts. Indicators are drawn from Twigg's resilience characteristics framework, UNDRR scorecards, and field-tested ABA tools. Enables before-after and longitudinal comparison across target zones.

## Indicator Domains

1. **Governance and institutional capacity** — local risk governance, policy integration
2. **Risk knowledge** — community hazard awareness, early warning access
3. **Risk reduction measures** — physical, social, and economic DRR measures in place
4. **Preparedness and response** — contingency plans, trained responders, stockpiles
5. **Recovery capacity** — financial reserves, social networks, adaptive capacity
