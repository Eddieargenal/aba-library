# Sendai Municipal DRR Progress Review

## Purpose

Links ABA programme outcomes to Sendai Framework global DRR targets at the municipal level. Enables local governments and humanitarian partners to report progress toward Sendai Targets A–G using data generated by ABA assessments and field instruments.

## Sendai Targets Overview

<!-- TODO[agent]: Summarize each Target (A–G) with the municipal-level indicator and data source in the wiki -->

- **Target A**: Reduce global disaster mortality
- **Target B**: Reduce number of affected people
- **Target C**: Reduce direct economic loss
- **Target D**: Reduce disaster damage to critical infrastructure
- **Target E**: Increase number of countries with national/local DRR strategies
- **Target F**: Increase international cooperation for developing countries
- **Target G**: Increase access to early warning and risk information
