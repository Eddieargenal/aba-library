# Area-Based MEL Framework

## Purpose

Provides the overall monitoring, evaluation, and learning architecture for area-based humanitarian and DRR programmes. Defines what to measure, at what frequency, and how learning feeds back into programme adaptation. Distinguishes area-level outcomes from household or sectoral metrics.

## Core MEL Questions

1. Is the area-based approach reducing vulnerability for the most at-risk populations within the target zone?
2. Are local systems (WASH, shelter, health, markets) recovering or strengthening over time?
3. Is the ABA enabling better coordination and reduced duplication?

- **Input monitoring**: Resources deployed per zone, actor coverage

- **Output tracking**: Services delivered, infrastructure restored, households reached
- **Outcome measurement**: Vulnerability reduction, service functionality, resilience indicators
- **Learning loops**: After-action reviews, adaptive management triggers
