# Tool 13: Area Coordination Dashboard

## Purpose
A dashboard for tracking coordination status in real time during implementation — including actor activities, geographic coverage, emerging gaps, duplications, referral completions, and coordination meeting decisions. Supports active coordination platform management.

## Decision questions
- Are all planned activities being implemented on schedule?
- Are new gaps or duplications emerging that require coordination response?
- Are referral pathways functioning as designed?
