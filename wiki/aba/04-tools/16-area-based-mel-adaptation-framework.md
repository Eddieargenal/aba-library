# Tool 16: Area Based Mel Adaptation Framework

## Purpose
A monitoring, evaluation, and learning framework for area-based programs that tracks area-level outcomes, systems changes, DRR progress, and community resilience indicators across all sectors and lifecycle stages. Defines adaptive management triggers.

## Decision questions
- Are area-level outcomes being achieved across sectors?
- Is DRR integration producing measurable risk reduction outcomes?
- What evidence should trigger program adaptation or course correction?
