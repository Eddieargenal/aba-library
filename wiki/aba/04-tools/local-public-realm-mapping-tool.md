<a id="purpose"></a>
## Purpose

Mapping the four components of the local public realm (public spaces, service/civic centres, third spaces, civic media) provides a structured diagnostic for neighbourhood anchors and service gaps, grounding neighbourhood definition in observable infrastructure.

<a id="decision-questions"></a>
## Decision Questions

Field query this should answer: *"How do I map the physical and social anchors of a neighbourhood to identify service gaps and community assets?"*
