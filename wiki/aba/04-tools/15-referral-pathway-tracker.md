# Tool 15: Referral Pathway Tracker

## Purpose
A tracker for managing referrals between actors and sectors within the area, ensuring beneficiaries who need services from multiple actors are identified, referred, and tracked through to service completion.

## Decision questions
- Are referral pathways functioning and are referred beneficiaries receiving services?
- Where in the referral chain are cases being lost or delayed?
- Which referral pathways require strengthening or new actor engagement?
