# 12 — Risks and Contradictions

Critical analysis layer covering known contradictions, weak evidence claims, protection risks, and unresolved questions. Consult this section before generating any recommendation — it surfaces where the literature conflicts, where evidence is thin, and where implementation has failed. One file is fully populated; the rest are stubs.

## Documents

| Document | Description |
|---|---|
| [[protection-and-do-no-harm-risks]] | **What:** A risk register cataloguing protection risks specific to area-based approaches — geographic targeting, community mapping, data sharing, and local power structures. **Why:** ABA's geographic and participatory design creates specific exposure risks that generic do-no-harm checklists miss. **When/How:** Screen against this register before programme design and at each implementation stage; brief field teams on these risks before community engagement. |
| [[area-targeting-protection-risk]] | Risk that geographic targeting creates protection risks — enabling looting, beneficiary exclusion, stigmatization of labelled areas, and inter-community tensions at boundaries. |
