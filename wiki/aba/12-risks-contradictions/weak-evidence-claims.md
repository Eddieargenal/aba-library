# Weak Evidence Claims

## Purpose

Identifies claims that are widely repeated in the urban ABA/DRR literature but rest on thin or problematic evidence. Prevents agents from presenting popular assertions as established facts. Each entry notes what the claim is, how it is typically cited, what the actual evidence base is, and what would be needed to strengthen it.
