# Unresolved Questions in Urban ABA/DRR

## Purpose

Catalogues substantive open questions that the existing literature has not resolved. Signals to agents and users where current knowledge is incomplete and where confidence in recommendations should be low.

### Question Categories

#### Area Definition
- How large should an ABA zone be to be analytically meaningful vs operationally manageable?
- What happens to households at zone boundaries — are they systematically under-served?

#### Vulnerability Measurement
- Can a single composite vulnerability score replace multi-dimensional analysis without losing critical information?
- How do gender and disability intersect with geographic vulnerability in urban settings?

#### Coordination Effectiveness
- What coordination arrangements actually reduce overlap vs those that only appear to?
- How do informal power dynamics among agencies undermine formal coordination structures?

#### DRR Integration
- At what point in crisis-to-recovery does DRR integration become possible vs counterproductive?
- What minimal municipal governance capacity is needed for DRR integration to take hold?

#### Scale
- Does ABA quality degrade at scale? What is the evidence?
