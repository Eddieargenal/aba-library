<a id="purpose"></a>
## Purpose

Turn the generic Characteristics into a **locally valid** set — fitted to the area's hazards, culture, and capacities. *"The global characteristics don't fit our village — how do we rewrite them?"*

<a id="decision-questions"></a>
## Decision Questions

- Which characteristics are irrelevant, missing, or differently expressed here?
- Who in the community defines what "resilient" means in this context?

<a id="method"></a>
## Method

Work through the Characteristics with community members, dropping/adding/rewording to fit local reality (the source's Box 5 Philippines example is the model). **Gaps:** no step-by-step contextual-analysis protocol; examples limited to rural settings.

<a id="risks"></a>
## Risks

Adaptation captured by facilitators rather than the community; in conflict settings, see R-resilience-conflict-gap before customising.
