<a id="purpose"></a>
## Purpose

Establish where a community sits **today** on the path to disaster resilience, using the Characteristics framework as the reference. *"How do I measure how resilient our community is right now?"*

<a id="decision-questions"></a>
## Decision Questions

- Which characteristics are present, partial, or absent now?
- What is the baseline against which progress (T-measure-resilience-progress) will be tracked?

<a id="method"></a>
## Method

Combine the participatory VCA analysis (T-conduct-vca-with-characteristics) with a characteristic-by-characteristic current-state read. **Gaps:** no validated quantitative scoring/weighting; urban and conflict adaptation absent (see R-resilience-conflict-gap).

<a id="risks"></a>
## Risks

Presenting a partial, qualitative read as a definitive score; ignoring the documented urban/conflict gaps.
