# Coordination Decision Log Template

## Purpose
A template for logging all coordination decisions made at area coordination meetings, including the rationale, responsible actor, deadline, and implementation status. Provides accountability and institutional memory across actor turnover.

## Template

| Decision # | Date | Decision | Rationale | Responsible Actor | Deadline | Status (open/in progress/closed) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 001 | 2026-MM-DD | [Decision text] | [Why this was decided] | [Organization name] | [Date] | open | |

## Usage notes
- Number decisions sequentially within each area program
- Archive closed decisions but keep them visible
- Review open decisions at every coordination meeting
- Flag decisions overdue by more than 2 weeks
