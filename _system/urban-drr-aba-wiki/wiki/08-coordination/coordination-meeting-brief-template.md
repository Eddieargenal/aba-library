# Area Coordination Meeting Brief Template

## Purpose
A template for area coordination meeting briefs, standardizing the format for agenda, attendance, decisions, action points, and gap/duplication reporting across all coordination meetings in the area.

## Template structure

**MEETING HEADER**
- Date, time, location
- Facilitator and note-taker
- Area / neighborhood name
- Meeting number (sequential)

**ATTENDANCE**
- Organization | Representative | Role

**AGENDA**
1. Previous action points review
2. 5W update and gap/duplication report
3. [Specific agenda items]
4. New action points
5. Next meeting

**DECISION LOG**
| Decision # | Decision | Rationale | Responsible | Deadline |

**ACTION POINTS**
| # | Action | Responsible | Deadline | Status |

**GAP/DUPLICATION REPORT**
| Need/Activity | Area | Population | Gap or Duplication | Proposed action | Assigned to |

**NEXT MEETING**
Date, time, location, proposed agenda items
