# Summary

Parker and Maynard's 2015 IIED review examines how area-based approaches have been used in humanitarian response to urban crises, analyzing case studies to identify what makes an approach genuinely area-based versus simply geographically located. The review establishes three core criteria for an area-based approach: geographic targeting, multi-sectoral integration, and participatory engagement. It is the primary reference for defining ABA and distinguishing appropriate from inappropriate use of the term.
