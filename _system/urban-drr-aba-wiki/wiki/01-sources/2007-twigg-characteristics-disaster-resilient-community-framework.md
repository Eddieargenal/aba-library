# Summary

This guidance note by John Twigg, developed for the DFID DRR Interagency Coordination Group, presents a detailed framework of the characteristics of a disaster-resilient community organized across five thematic components. It is widely used in humanitarian and DRR programming to design community engagement activities, technical interventions, and monitoring indicators. The framework connects community-level resilience to broader DRR systems including governance, knowledge, risk assessment, and preparedness.
