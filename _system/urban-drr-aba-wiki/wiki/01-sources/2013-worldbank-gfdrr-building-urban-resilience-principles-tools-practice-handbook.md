# Summary

This World Bank and GFDRR handbook provides principles, tools, and practical guidance for building resilience in cities, targeting local government leaders, urban planners, and development practitioners. It presents risk screening frameworks for prioritizing neighborhoods based on hazard exposure and vulnerability, city-level investment logic for resilience programming, and guidance on integrating resilience into municipal systems and plans. The handbook bridges DRR investment planning with urban development and humanitarian response design.
