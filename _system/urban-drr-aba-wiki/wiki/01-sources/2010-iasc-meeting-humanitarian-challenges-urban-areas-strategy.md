# Summary

This IASC strategy document establishes the inter-agency framework for responding to humanitarian crises in urban settings, addressing the unique coordination, programming, and protection challenges posed by cities. It sets out strategic priorities for improving humanitarian response in urban areas including better analysis, more coherent coordination mechanisms, and stronger links to local governance. The strategy is a foundational reference for area-based coordination and cluster-municipal alignment in urban response design.
