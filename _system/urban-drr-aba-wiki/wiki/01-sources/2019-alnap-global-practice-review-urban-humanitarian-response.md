# Summary

ALNAP's Global Practice Review 12 synthesizes global evidence and lessons from urban humanitarian response across multiple crises and contexts, providing practitioners with evidence-based guidance on protection mainstreaming, sector technical design, implementation sequencing, market-based programming, and MEL in urban settings. As the most comprehensive global evidence synthesis on urban humanitarian response, it is referenced across five matrix tools and provides the evidence foundation for quality standards in urban programming. The GPR12 is a critical source for ensuring technical quality in area-based programs.
