# Definition
The probability of harmful outcomes (loss, damage, disruption) arising from the interaction of hazards with the exposed and vulnerable elements of the urban system.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
Urban risk is shaped by rapid urbanization, informal settlements, weak governance, and compounding vulnerabilities that require systematic, area-level analysis rather than generic risk assessments.
