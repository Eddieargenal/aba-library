# Definition
The systematic comparison of response options across sectors (shelter, WASH, cash, livelihoods, health, protection) and modalities (direct delivery, cash, vouchers, technical assistance, advocacy, referral) to identify the most appropriate combination for a given area, population, and crisis context.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
Urban crises rarely have single-sector solutions — multi-sector analysis prevents programming silos and enables integrated area strategies that address root causes rather than treating symptoms independently.
