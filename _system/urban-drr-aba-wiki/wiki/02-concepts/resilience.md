# Definition
The ability of individuals, communities, urban systems, and institutions to absorb shocks, adapt to change, and recover from crises while maintaining essential functions and progressively transforming conditions toward reduced risk.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
ABA programs should contribute to long-term resilience — but resilience is a contested concept with many competing definitions and measurement approaches, and programs that use it without operationalizing it risk producing vague outcomes that cannot be measured.
