# Definition
The formal and informal institutional arrangements through which local governments identify, assess, manage, and communicate risk, including DRR planning, land use zoning, building standards, early warning systems, and emergency management.

# Why it matters for urban DRR and response
ABA programs must engage with — and potentially strengthen — municipal risk governance to ensure sustainability and alignment with local authority mandates; bypassing municipalities creates parallel systems that undermine long-term risk reduction.
