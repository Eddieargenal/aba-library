# Tool 11: Sector Technical Design Checklist

## Purpose
A quality checklist for reviewing the technical design of each sector intervention to ensure it meets sector standards, integrates DRR principles, includes local actor roles, specifies targeting methodology, and defines handover arrangements.

## Decision questions
- Does the shelter design meet minimum Sphere standards for urban contexts?
- Is DRR integrated into WASH design (e.g. flood-resilient latrines)?
- Are local service providers engaged in service delivery design?
