# Stage 0: Appropriateness Decision

## Stage purpose
Determine whether an area-based approach is appropriate, necessary, and feasible for this crisis. This is the gateway decision that should precede any ABA program design. Teams that skip this stage risk committing to an ABA design that does not fit the context.
